About Me

Who am I

Hiya! I’m Lucas! A Year 1 student in Singapore Poly, pursuing a Diploma in Infocomm Security Management (DISM). I graduated from St. Patrick’s School and applied for the course through the Early Admission Exercise (EAE) in Secondary 4.

I enjoy exploring many aspects of technology, both in software and hardware. With coding being so individualised, it allows me to explore many ways to solving a single problem. I hope to learn more about technology in my 3 years at Singapore Poly to be proficient enough to excel in my education and career in the future. Besides that, if I’m not busy with work, you’ll usually find me playing video games or watching a show. You can read more about my hobbies below!

Personality

I am an INTJ from the Myers Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) test! I prefer to recharge in quieter environments and analytically observe a situation before making a sound judgment. I also favour looking at the practicality of an idea instead of the potential it presents, making sure a project is worth investing time in. Always ensuring that my time is well-spent as it is the thing I treasure the most.

Interests & Hobbies

I love gaming, playing sports, video editing and spending time with my friends. So I created a video to showcase all the hobbies and interests I have.

Here is a short summary of the many hobbies and interests I enjoy!

  • I've played video games since I started Primary School, mainly playing First Person Shooter (FPS) games like Counter-Strike Global Offensive. I even played competitively in 2018 for the Testament Cup.
  • I enjoy all kinds of team sports, like volleyball and football. But I like volleyball the most, having met many great people who share the same love for the sport.
  • I also love cycling; having begun cycling in 2013, I cycled at East Coast Park at least once a week.
  • Lastly, I enjoy video editing. Using software like Sony Vegas, then After Effects, I edit videos mainly targeted toward games I play and the shows I watch.
