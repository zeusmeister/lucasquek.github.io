Timed Math Quiz

This was a math game created by my group of friends, Elliot, Xavier, Alfred and I. It was made to help train mental sums by giving mathematical equations consisting of random numbers and is available for the 4 basic mathematical operators. It also allows for the user to change the playtime and limit the difficulty.

The game was made with python and we hope to incoporate the game into a website/game in the future.
